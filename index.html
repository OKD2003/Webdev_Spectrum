Advancing medicine
through research

PicnicHealth selectively partners with researchers on specific disease areas, so peaople with those medical conditions are
eligible to contribute their records to ressearch. By allowing these researchers to use anonymzed rexords, PicnicHealth is
able to provide study participants their medical record timelines for free.

We anonymize all records

We dont just redact your records by crossing out or deleting your name. when preparing records to share with researchers, we actully remove specific pieces of informations from your records-like lab results or medications-and put those into a separate file.

We structure informations into a single a dataset.

We combine your de-identified data with similar data from other patients—so it’s most impactful for researchers and private for you.

Researchers use those datasets with the hope of finding new breakthroughs.

Most of our datasets feature de-identified data from thousands of patients. This protects your privacy. And it enables scientists to quickly identify trends and unlock insights that could lead to discoveries—or even a cure.
